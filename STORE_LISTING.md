## Full description — opener ✅ APPROVED (also the in-app `app_description`)
> The apps on your phone are talking to trackers behind your back. Shutter cuts them off — quietly, on your device, with nothing sent to any server. And when blocking trips an app up, Shutter's the only one that tells you which one, and fixes it in a tap.

**To expand for the Play full-description field at ship** — true selling points to draw on (no overclaiming):
- Blocks trackers and ads with an on-device filter (StevenBlack hosts).
- Nothing leaves your phone — no remote server, no account.
- The "what broke?" nudge names the app + host and offers one-tap "let it through".
- "Apps you've allowed" screen with one-tap undo.
- No root required.
- Light on battery (local filtering).
- Open-source.
